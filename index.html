Corynebacterium species Pangenomics

KLemonLab

Metabolic capabilities are highly conserved among human nasal-associated Corynebacterium species Supplemental Methods

This repository includes:

  • Link to bioRxiv: https://www.biorxiv.org/content/10.1101/2023.06.05.543719v1.full

  • The raw .fasta files for initial input, provided here.

  • Supplemental methods for program installations.

  • Supplemental methods for Prokka v1.14.6 annotation of 107 Corynebacterium strain genomes.

  • Supplemental methods of pangenome analyses performed with GET_HOMOLOGUES, GET_PHYLOMARKERS and IQ-TREE2.

  • Supplemental methods for the anvi’o v7 pangenomic and metabolic analysis.

  • Supplemental methods of COG anvi’o annotated gene clusters assignments to the persistent or accessory genome with PPanGGOLiN analysis.

  • Supplemental methods for COG functional analysis based on PPanGGOLiN partitions:

    • Corynebacterium propinquum

    • Corynebacterium pseudodiphtheriticum

    • Corynebacterium accolens

    • Corynebacterium tuberculostearicum

  • Supplemental methods for the Glycogen analysis.
